About Me

Hello, I'm Leslie!


Background

Originally from the Bay Area, I currently reside in sunny San Diego working remotely full-time as a UX Designer. I graduated from UC San Diego in 2019 in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Design & Interaction. However, I am no stranger to coding! I have a 3 year background in computer science before switching to design.


Passionate about

I found design much more satisfying knowing that I'd be able to make a bigger impact and influence on user-centered issues. As a natural problem solver, there is nothing better than improving something that would help make someone's life better! Having grown up in a low-income community, I want to give back and help those who also face the same struggles as I did. Today, I love designing for all types of users, but feel best advocating for accessibility and access in underrepresented communities.
