InEight

Background


What


InEight is a construction project management software. InEight offers easy, quick solutions for keeping projects in schedule, budgeting, planning, and building innovative future infrastructure. Time Phased Forecast is a Forecasting tool letting users break down projected values into smaller, manageable time periods and calculations. I collaborated on this project closely with the product team, engineers, and UX team.




Problem Statement


Users need to see all past, current, and future values for their projects in various sectors, known commonly as Time Phased Forecasting. Manually adjusting cost item values to reflect accurately for project planning lacked the visibility for users since the feature had limited abilities.




Solution


Revamping the entire Time Phased Forecast with a comprehensive grid with an up-to-date design system and adding new TPF features (Manual grid override, Push to Live) gives users flexibility and better accurary for managing their Control budget and plans.




Timeframe


June 2022 - August 2023 (22.6 - 23.8)


Role


Lead UX/UI Interaction Designer for Control
